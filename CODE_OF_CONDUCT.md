# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity,
gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal
appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive in communication
- Giving and accepting helpful feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community and project

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of
acceptable behavior and may take appropriate action in response to violations.

## Reporting

Please report unacceptable behavior by opening a private security/contact issue
or contacting project maintainers through repository channels.
